# Master's in Data Science & Artificial Intelligence

The objective of this repository is to act as my personal knowledge base, learning journal, and labs for my Master's Degree in Data Science and AI.

## Disclaimer

While individual course deliveries and major projects live in their respective standalone repositories, this monorepo captures my daily evolution, core concepts, research notes, and small scale experiments.
Those standalone repositories often involve collaboration with my classmates, as academic projects are designed to be implemented in groups. This space, however, tracks my individual growth and technical foundation.

## Reposority Structure

Every trimester have a folder per course, is tructured to separate theoretical understanding from hands-on practice:

```
master-data-science-ai
├── README.md
└── trimester-1
    ├── mcdi500
    │   ├── labs
    │   ├── notes
    │   ├── project
    │   └── README.md
    └── mcdi501
        ├── labs
        ├── notes
        ├── project
        └── README.md
```

Where:

- `notes/`: Markdown summaries, mathematical formulations, and cheatsheets.
- `labs/`: Jupyter Notebooks, raw scripts, and data pipelines built during lectures.
- `project/`: Links (Git submodules) to the official academic deliverables.
